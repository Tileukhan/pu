About us

History

MR.COOK is a culinary gem with a rich history dating back to 2003. Founded by Seifulla Tileukhan,a visionary chef with a passion for creating exceptional dining experiences, our restaurant has been a beloved part of the Astana dining scene for 5 years.Over the years, MR.COOK has evolved while remaining true to its roots, blending tradition and innovation to create a menu that showcases the best of Western cuisine. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and creativity has earned us a reputation as one of the finest dining establishments in Astana.

Chiefs

At MR.COOK, our kitchen is led by a team of exceptionally talented chefs, each with their own unique culinary expertise. Head Chef Tileukhan brings 5 years of culinary experience, having trained in renowned kitchens around the world. Our chefs are dedicated to crafting dishes that not only tantalize the taste buds but also engage the senses in a delightful gastronomic journey. Whether it's Tileukhan's signature beshbarmak or the creative innovations of our talented sous chefs, our culinary team is committed to exceeding your culinary expectations. Join us at MR.COOK for a dining experience that combines tradition, innovation, and the art of gastronomy. We look forward to serving you and making your visit truly exceptional.
